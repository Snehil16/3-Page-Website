WHICH FEMALES AND GIRLS WILL BE AFFECTED BY THE HUNGER CRISIS?
Girls, boys, women, and men are all affected differently by hunger. Girls frequently consume less food and eat last when food is limited. 70% of those who are hungry across the world are women and girls. Due to the accelerated rate of their growth and physical change, adolescents and young children below the age of five are more prone to malnutrition.

Food Security Update
Worldwide, domestic inflation in food prices is still very high. Nearly all low- and middle-income countries have experienced high inflation, according to data from May to August 2022; 93.3 percent of low-income nations, 90.9 percent of relatively low nations, and 93 % of top nations have experienced inflationary pressures above five percent, with several experiencing double-digit inflation. With around 85.7 percent of high-income nations suffering high food price inflation, the proportion of countries with a high inflation has also dramatically grown.
